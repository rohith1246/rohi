The Science: How Rohi Solves the Habit Loop

Habits are driven by a neurological loop: Trigger ➔ Urge ➔ Action ➔ Reward. Standard apps only track the result, leaving you to fight the urge alone. Rohi actively breaks the cycle at every single stage.

1
The Cue / Trigger

The Problem: Triggers occur automatically in specific contexts or emotional states.

Rohi's Solution: Pattern Predictor

Daily AI Nudges evaluate context logs to identify your risk times and trigger patterns, notifying you before they strike.

2
The Craving / Urge

The Problem: A chemical craving spike is physically overwhelming, driving immediate action.

Rohi's Solution: Urge Surfing Wave

Paced Box Breathing regulates physiology, while custom AI distraction plans formulate immediate physical and cognitive reframing statements.

3
The Action / Routine

The Problem: The brain falls back to automated negative habits (scrolling, smoking, eating).

Rohi's Solution: CBT AI Coach

An on-demand CBT companion adapts its response tone to redirect your routine into healthy alternative habits.

4
The Reward

The Problem: Breaking streaks deletes progress, causing shame, which triggers the loop again.

Rohi's Solution: Virtual Garden

Successful days grow animated trees. Slipping pauses growth rather than resetting the garden—removing shame and reinforcing positive habits.

Deep-Dive: How Rohi Works Under the Hood

Explore the advanced mechanics that separate Rohi from typical utility trackers.

🛡️ Zero-Trace Localized Security

Rohi values your recovery privacy. Every session uses custom security headers, CSRF request shields, and XSS sanitizers to prevent data leaks. Passwords are saved as secure PBKDF2 hashes.

🌊 Physiological Urge Surfing

Cravings typically peak within 10 to 15 minutes. The paced breathing guide slows heart rate variability, signaling safety to the amygdala, while the AI redirect suggests active physical tasks to pass the peak of the wave.

🧠 Tone-Adaptive Generative Support

Rather than static replies, Rohi queries database logs in real time. If the system detects high stress levels or frequent relapses, it alters the Generative AI's prompt constraints to provide a nurturing, non-judgmental CBT counseling tone.
