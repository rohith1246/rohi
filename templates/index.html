Virtual Recovery Garden

Punitive counters that reset to zero on relapses cause guilt and discouragement. Instead, Rohi grows a virtual tree. Successful days advance your tree through 6 visual growth stages (Seed to Bloom). Slips merely pause growth without destroying your achievements.

  • Animated SVG trees reflecting progress
  • Growth Paused states for self-compassion
  • Grid layout for tracking multiple habits

CBT Adaptive AI Coach

Talk confidentially with Rohi Coach, a virtual Cognitive Behavioral Therapy companion. Rohi reads your past logs and chats to maintain memory, dynamically adjusting its tone (nurturing, strict, motivational, or therapeutic) to match your current stress levels.

  • CBT-informed guidance on-demand
  • Context-aware sentiment memory
  • Zero-dependency dual fallback architecture

Predictive Daily Nudges

Proactive recovery starts before triggers hit. Rohi evaluates your historical log patterns, emotional states, and timing windows, then outputs a custom daily nudge warning you of risks and suggesting actionable, budget-friendly CBT redirects.

  • Daily dashboard notification banners
  • Customized redirects in local currency
  • Automated logs pattern parser

When cravings peak, access emergency tools immediately. Synchronize your breathing with our Box Breathing visualizer, and type in your triggers to generate an on-demand AI survival plan outlining physical redirects and cognitive reframing steps.

  • Interactive guided breathing visualizer
